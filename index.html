Who We Are

About GreenVion

GreenVion is a sustainable façade innovation focused on retrofitting existing buildings with bio-integrated modular façade systems. Designed for warm climates, the solution helps reduce indoor heat gain, lower dependency on air-conditioning, and improve environmental performance.

Vision

To transform existing buildings into climate-responsive, energy-efficient environments through scalable façade innovation.

Mission

To create retrofit solutions that improve comfort, reduce cooling loads, and support sustainable urban development.

"Buildings are not passive boxes — they can breathe, shade, and cool themselves."

GreenVion Philosophy

Challenges We Address

Warm-climate buildings face compounding pressures that drive up energy consumption and carbon emissions.

☀️

High solar heat gain through building envelopes

❄️

Excessive dependence on air-conditioning systems

⚡

🏙️

Urban heat island effect amplifying ambient temperatures

🌿

Carbon emissions from cooling-intensive buildings

Our Innovation

GreenVion Bio-Integrated Façade System

A modular façade retrofit solution integrating passive cooling principles with vegetation and shading strategies.

Features

  • 🧩 Modular installation for existing buildings
  • 🌱 Integrated planting system for natural cooling
  • 💨 Shading and ventilation enhancement
  • 🪟 Adaptable to windows and façade openings
  • 🔧 Low-maintenance retrofit approach
